📖 How to read this dashboard
  1. Overview: Upon loading, the dashboard displays the GANTT timeline and execution status metrics for all AI Zones across various PLC (Project Life Cycle) stages.
  2. Filters: Use the dropdown filters at the top (Region, Customer, Chip Type, etc.) to dynamically narrow down your search. Click "Reset Filters" to clear.
  3. GANTT Bars: Each bar indicates the duration from Preflight Start to Launch. The bar is divided by the FHO date in the middle:
    • Left translucent bar segment: Preflight Start to FHO date.
    • Right solid bar segment: FHO date to Launch.
  4. Details tooltip: Hover your mouse cursor over any GANTT bar to view a details card (BDP Useable MW, Site Type, Contract Status, Customer, etc.).
  5. PLC Stages Color Coding: See the legend at the bottom of the page to match colors with stages (Launched, Execution, Planning, etc.).
